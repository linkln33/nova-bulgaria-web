NOVA Bulgaria Whitepaper

Version 1.0 - February 2025

Abstract

NOVA Bulgaria represents a paradigm shift in nation-state architecture, implementing a fully decentralized digital sovereign nation powered by blockchain technology. This whitepaper outlines the technical foundation, governance mechanisms, and economic model that enables a new form of digital citizenship and national organization.

Key innovations include:

  • Blockchain-based national identity system with cryptographic proof of citizenship
  • Democratic governance through smart contracts
  • Native digital currency (BGL) with advanced economic policies
  • Proof of Contribution mechanism for value creation and distribution

Consensus Mechanism

NOVA Bulgaria utilizes a hybrid consensus mechanism combining Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS) with Proof of Contribution (PoC):

  • Validator selection based on stake and contribution score
  • Dynamic validator set with maximum of 100 validators
  • Slashing conditions for malicious behavior
  • Block time of 5 seconds with instant finality

Voting Mechanism

The voting power is calculated using a quadratic formula that considers both token holdings and contribution score:


func CalculateVotingPower(tokens sdk.Int, contributionScore sdk.Dec) sdk.Dec {
    return sdk.NewDec(tokens.Int64()).Sqrt().
           Add(contributionScore.Sqrt()).
           Quo(sdk.NewDec(2))
}
