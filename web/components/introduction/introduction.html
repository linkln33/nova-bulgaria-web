Why Blockchain?

Understanding Blockchain (For Beginners)

Blockchain is a special kind of digital record-keeping system where information is stored across many computers instead of a single one. Imagine a diary that everyone can see, but no one can change or erase once something is written. This system is extremely secure, transparent, and cannot be controlled by a single person or government.

Key Features of Blockchain:
  • Decentralization: No central authority controls it. Everyone in the network has a copy of the data.
  • Transparency: Transactions are visible to everyone, ensuring fairness and preventing corruption.
  • Security: Once data is recorded, it cannot be altered or erased.
  • Smart Contracts: These are self-executing programs that automatically follow rules without needing a middleman (e.g., contracts, payments, and voting systems can all be automated).
