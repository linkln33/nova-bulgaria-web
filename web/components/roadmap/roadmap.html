NOVA BULGARIA Roadmap

A decade-by-decade plan for rebuilding Bulgaria through blockchain technology, meritocracy, and direct democracy

Foundation Phase (2025-2034)

During this critical first decade, we will establish the core infrastructure of NOVA BULGARIA, focusing on building the blockchain foundation, governance systems, and initial community.

2025-2026

Inception & Foundation

  • • Launch of NOVA BULGARIA blockchain on Cosmos ecosystem
  • • Development of Constitution smart contracts
  • • Initial BGL token distribution (0.1% annual - 1B BGL/year)
  • • Creation of digital identity system for citizens
2027-2028

Governance Systems

  • • Implementation of Decentralized Autonomous Governance (DAG)
  • • Launch of citizen-proposed law system
  • • Development of blockchain courts for dispute resolution
  • • Introduction of Proof-of-Contribution (PoC) voting system
2029-2030

Economic Foundation

  • • Launch of BGL-Stable for everyday transactions
  • • Creation of decentralized financial system
  • • Implementation of transparent treasury system
  • • Development of citizen-owned economic growth model
2031-2032

Core Sector Development

  • • Launch of initial 8 core sector parachains
  • • Implementation of healthcare, education, and agriculture modules
  • • Development of energy and transportation infrastructure
  • • Creation of manufacturing and tourism blockchain frameworks
2033-2034

Community Expansion

  • • Global outreach to Bulgarian diaspora
  • • Implementation of digital citizenship program
  • • Development of education and training systems
  • • Establishment of meritocratic advancement pathways

Development Phase (2035-2044)

The second decade focuses on expanding NOVA BULGARIA's infrastructure, implementing all core and new sectors, and increasing token distribution to support growth.

2035-2036

Infrastructure Expansion

  • • Launch of remaining 16 core sector parachains
  • • Integration of all government services on blockchain
  • • Increased token distribution (0.2% annual - 2B BGL/year)
  • • Development of advanced governance mechanisms
2037-2038

New Sector Development

  • • Launch of initial 10 new sector parachains
  • • Implementation of blockchain, esoteric, and religions modules
  • • Development of space technology and quantum computing sectors
  • • Integration of biotechnology and renewable energy frameworks
2039-2040

Global Integration

  • • Establishment of international partnerships
  • • Integration with global blockchain networks
  • • Development of cross-chain interoperability
  • • Launch of international digital citizenship program
2041-2042

Complete Sector Implementation

  • • Launch of remaining 10 new sector parachains
  • • Implementation of supernatural, digital finance, and sustainable development modules
  • • Full integration of all 44 parachains
  • • Development of cross-sector collaboration frameworks
2043-2044

Transition to Development Phase

  • • Preparation for increased token distribution
  • • Evaluation and optimization of all systems
  • • Development of advanced governance mechanisms
  • • Planning for the Development Phase (2045-2084)

Future Vision (2045+)

Beyond the first two decades, NOVA BULGARIA will continue to evolve and expand, with increased token distribution and advanced development phases.

  • •

    Focus on technological advancement and innovation

  • •

    Expansion of global influence and partnerships

    Advanced societal and economic development

    Maturation of all systems and frameworks

Expansion & Peak Phases (2085-2124)

    Seventh Decade: 1.5% annual (15B BGL/year)

    Eighth Decade: 2.0% annual (20B BGL/year)

    Global expansion and influence

  • •

    Peak Phase (2115-2124):

    Final Decade: 3.0% annual (30B BGL/year)

    Completion of token distribution

    Full maturation of NOVA BULGARIA

Long-Term Vision

NOVA BULGARIA aims to become a fully realized decentralized nation, serving as a model for governance, economic freedom, and societal organization worldwide.

Governance

A fully decentralized, transparent, and meritocratic system where citizens directly control all aspects of governance.

Economy

A self-sustaining, corruption-free economic system that rewards contribution and eliminates financial inequality.

Society

A global community united by shared values of transparency, fairness, and collective progress.
