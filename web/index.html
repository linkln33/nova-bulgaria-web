Whitepaper v1.1

1. Vision

NOVA BULGARIA is a revolutionary initiative to create a sovereign digital nation on its own blockchain, forked from Cosmos. The nation will be governed by principles of meritocracy, Proof of Contribution (PoC), and decentralization, ensuring that governance, economy, and social systems are fair, transparent, and community-driven.

The project honors Bulgaria's history and spirit, symbolized through the national currency — the Bulgarian Lion (BGL), a powerful representation of strength, independence, and unity.

2. Blockchain Infrastructure

NOVA BULGARIA will run on a custom-built blockchain, forked from the Cosmos SDK. Cosmos was chosen for its flexibility, scalability, and interoperability, allowing NOVA BULGARIA to remain sovereign while connecting to other ecosystems.

⚡ Why Cosmos?

  • Modular Design: Easily adaptable to NOVA BULGARIA's unique governance and identity systems.
  • Interoperability: Seamless connection with other blockchains for trade, diplomacy, and collaboration.
  • Scalability & Speed: Fast transactions and low fees, essential for handling large-scale governance and social interactions.

🛠️ Technical Stack:

  • Consensus: Tendermint (Byzantine Fault Tolerant)
  • Governance: On-chain voting with PoC-weighted influence
  • Smart Contracts: CosmWasm for flexible, secure contract execution
  • Storage: On-chain for public data, IPFS/Arweave for private information

3. The Bulgarian Lion (BGL) — National Currency

The national currency, BGL (Bulgarian Lion), symbolizes courage, strength, and national pride. BGL will be a blockchain-native token, earned through real contributions to the nation — whether through labor, innovation, or social impact.

  • Proof of Contribution (PoC) Minting: New BGL tokens are minted and distributed based on verified contributions.
  • Stable Economic Model: The supply of BGL adjusts dynamically to reflect the community's growth and productivity.
  • Zero External Manipulation: As an independent blockchain, NOVA BULGARIA's economy is immune to external financial control or speculative manipulation.

4. Digital Citizenship & NFT ID System

Every citizen receives a Soulbound NFT ID — a non-transferable, evolving digital passport that serves as their identity and access key to the entire NOVA BULGARIA ecosystem.

🧠 NFT ID Features:

  • Non-Transferable: Permanently bound to the user's wallet to prevent fraud or identity theft.
  • Upgradable Metadata: Tracks qualifications, reputation, and rank, updating in real time as citizens contribute to society.
  • Access Key to the Ecosystem: The NFT ID grants access to governance systems, educational platforms, healthcare, and more.

5. Governance & Lawmaking

NOVA BULGARIA will run on a fully decentralized governance system, where all political decisions are made directly by qualified citizens through blockchain voting.

  • Direct Democracy: Every vote is recorded on-chain, and laws are executed automatically through smart contracts.
  • Expert-Based Voting: Citizens can only vote on proposals within their areas of expertise (tracked via NFT ID qualifications).
  • Real-Time Law Activation: Once a law passes with a 51% majority, it takes effect instantly — no bureaucracy, no delays.

6. Judicial & Law Enforcement System

  • Blockchain Courts: Cases are logged and processed transparently on-chain. Judges are selected based on their qualifications and reputation scores.
  • Decentralized Law Enforcement: Citizens can submit reports directly to the blockchain, and law enforcement actions are tracked for full transparency.

7. Platform & Social Ecosystem

The NOVA BULGARIA platform will be a social hub with multiple feeds for various aspects of governance, community interaction, and personal growth. Think of it as Facebook on steroids, tailored for nation-building.

  • Governance Feed: Propose, discuss, and vote on laws and policies.
  • Education Feed: Take exams, earn qualifications, and level up your NFT ID.
  • Community Feed: Organize local initiatives, volunteer for social projects, and track national progress.
