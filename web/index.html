Fund Allocation

Nova Bulgaria starts with the release of 1 million BGL in the first year, gradually increasing over the next 100 years to ensure controlled growth. The total supply of 1 trillion BGL is securely held in a smart contract and released in small portions based on economic demand and community participation. Instead of traditional mining or pre-allocated distribution, funds are unlocked through a Proof of Contribution (PoC) system, meaning individuals receive BGL based on their contributions to the nation's development—such as building projects, innovation, governance, or social services. As the economy grows and more people participate, the release rate gradually increases, ensuring there is always enough supply to support expansion while preventing inflation. This mechanism creates a self-sustaining economic model, where currency distribution is tied to real-world value and progress rather than speculation or centralized control. Over time, this ensures fairness, long-term stability, and an economy that adapts naturally to the needs of its citizens.

Vision for the Future

Rebuilding Bulgaria as a digital nation for all citizens

Why Bulgaria Needs Change

Bulgaria today faces serious challenges that affect the daily lives of its citizens. For decades, the same problems have persisted despite promises of change from traditional political parties.

Political Problems

  • • Government decisions favor the wealthy and connected
  • • Citizens have little say in important decisions
  • • Young people leave Bulgaria for better opportunities

Environmental Issues

  • • Beautiful natural resources are being damaged
  • • Pollution affects our health and quality of life
  • • Short-term profit is prioritized over long-term health

A New Approach

What if we could rebuild Bulgaria from the ground up? NOVA BULGARIA is a completely new way of thinking about our nation - one where citizens make the decisions, where transparency is guaranteed by technology, and where your contribution to society determines your influence, not your connections or wealth.

A Digital Nation Built By Its People

NOVA BULGARIA reimagines what a country can be in the digital age. Instead of power concentrated in the hands of a few politicians, we're creating a nation where all citizens participate directly in governance through secure digital technology.

Your Contribution Matters

In traditional systems, your voice is heard only during elections. In NOVA BULGARIA, your positive contributions to society are recognized and rewarded with greater influence. Whether you're a teacher, doctor, artist, or programmer, your expertise and effort directly translate to decision-making power in your field.

44 Areas of National Life

NOVA BULGARIA is organized into 44 specialized areas covering everything a nation needs to function. These include traditional sectors like Healthcare, Education, and Agriculture (24 sectors), plus new digital areas like Renewable Energy, Digital Identity, and Smart Cities (20 sectors). Each area has its own governance, allowing citizens with relevant expertise to make decisions in their fields.

How Our Digital Nation Works

NOVA BULGARIA uses advanced digital technology to ensure transparency, security, and fairness. Think of it as a digital record-keeping system that cannot be tampered with, allowing citizens to trust that everything from voting to resource allocation happens fairly.

Building a Complete Nation

Our 44 specialized areas work together to create a complete nation. Traditional sectors like Healthcare, Education, and Agriculture work alongside new digital sectors like Renewable Energy and Artificial Intelligence. Each area has dedicated funding and is governed by citizens with relevant expertise and contributions.

Citizens in Control

In NOVA BULGARIA, citizens make decisions directly rather than electing representatives who may not keep their promises. Our digital system ensures that everyone can participate, votes cannot be manipulated, and the results are implemented automatically.
