1. Vision

NOVA BULGARIA is a revolutionary initiative to create a fully decentralized, self-governed nation — where contribution defines citizenship, and blockchain ensures transparency and fairness. The project aims to transfer all traditionally government-controlled functions to the hands of national members through blockchain technology.

Governance, economy, healthcare, law enforcement, and public infrastructure will be managed transparently and immutably via blockchain, eliminating corruption and ensuring decisions are based on expertise and contribution.

2. Key Principles

🌟 Proof of Contribution

Members earn voting power, social rank, and economic benefits based on their real contributions — including skilled labor, innovation, and public service. Voting power updates daily, reflecting continuous personal development.

🆔 NFT ID System

A unique, evolving digital identity that serves as a passport to the nation. It records rank, expertise, reputation, and access rights within the ecosystem.

⚖️ Transparent Governance

Laws and policies are proposed, discussed, and voted on via blockchain. Decisions are executed immediately with 51% consensus, without bureaucratic delay.

💰 Dynamic Token Economy

A hybrid token system balancing long-term growth with short-term stability, designed to reward contribution while protecting against volatility.

3. Hybrid Token Model

🦁 BGL (Bulgarian Lion) — Core Nation Token

  • • Purpose: Governance, staking, long-term value accumulation, and PoC rewards
  • • Supply: Minted on demand for events (e.g., onboarding, project completion)
  • • Stability: Logarithmic supply curve slows token issuance as supply grows
  • • Price Floor: Supported by a treasury fund with auto-buy mechanism

🛡️ BGL-S (Stable Lion) — Native Stablecoin

  • • Purpose: Everyday transactions, salaries, and public services
  • • Peg: Basket of assets (fiat reserves, tokenized infrastructure, fractional BGL)
  • • Mechanism: Lock BGL to mint BGL-S, burn BGL-S to redeem
  • • Governance: Community can vote to adjust stabilization parameters

🎁 Event-Based Minting & Rewards

  • • Onboarding: 3 million BGL for new members upon registration
  • • Project Completion: BGL rewards for completed national projects
  • • Staking: Earn BGL-S dividends or project ownership shares

🚨 Emergency Stabilization

  • • Rapid Mint/Burn for crisis events
  • • Community-elected emergency committees
  • • Post-crisis audits for accountability
